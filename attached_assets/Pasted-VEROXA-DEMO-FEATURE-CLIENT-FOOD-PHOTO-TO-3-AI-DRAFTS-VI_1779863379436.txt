Do NOT connect real AI.
Do NOT connect real storage.
Do NOT connect Supabase.

Feature goal:
Inside the Client Portal demo, add a visual workflow that lets the user upload or select ONE food image locally and see Veroxa generate:
1. Three final post drafts
2. Caption text for each draft
3. Content angle for each draft
4. Platform recommendation
5. Suggested posting day/time
6. Visual schedule preview

This should feel like:
"Upload food picture → Veroxa AI prepares content → scheduled posts are ready."

Important:
This must be simulated demo AI only.
Use deterministic mock logic, not an AI API.

PART 5 — ROW LEVEL SECURITY PLANNING

Do NOT implement RLS yet.

But document future RLS rules in plain English:

Client:
- can view only their own client account data
- can upload media for their own account
- can view their own reports
- cannot see internal drafts, rejection notes, team notes, or other clients

Team:
- can view assigned clients
- can manage media review, drafts, scheduling, and weekly reports
- cannot manage billing or owner-level settings

Operator:
- can view all clients
- can approve monthly reports
- can view health/risk/alerts
- cannot change pricing or owner-level permissions

Owner:
- can view everything
- can manage users, pricing, account status, and business-level settings

System:
- can create activity logs, notifications, derived health fields, and automation-generated updates

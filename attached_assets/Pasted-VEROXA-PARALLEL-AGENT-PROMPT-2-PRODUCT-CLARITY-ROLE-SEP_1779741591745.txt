VEROXA CONTEXT

Veroxa is a SaaS-style restaurant marketing operating system.

It is not supposed to feel like a random agency dashboard.

It should feel like a clean operating system where every role knows exactly what to do.

Roles:

Client:
Uploads restaurant media, views calendar, sees reports, sends requests, manages account.

Team:
Executes work: media review, drafts, scheduling, weekly reports, alerts.

Operator:
Oversees quality, client health, risk, failed posts, monthly report approvals, team oversight.

Owner:
Sees revenue, growth, retention, critical alerts, client risk, and system health.

Locked Veroxa base pricing:

12-month plan: $997/mo
6-month plan: $1,097/mo
3-month plan: $1,197/mo
No-contract plan: $1,497/mo

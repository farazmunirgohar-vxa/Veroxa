CLIENT PORTAL CLEANUP — REMOVE INTERNAL OWNER/TEAM DETAILS

Objective:
Make the Client Portal feel client-facing, simple, and professional.

Rules:
- Do not remove Owner/Operator/Team portals
- Only clean the Client Portal
- Demo data only
- No backend changes
- No redesign
- Keep dark Veroxa theme

Client Portal should NOT show:
- Owner
- Operator
- Team
- Assigned role
- Assigned reviewer
- Internal notes
- AI confidence score
- Internal validation language
- Work queue language
- Any employee/team management details

Replace client-facing language:

Instead of “Owner: Team Review”
Use: “Status: In progress”

Instead of “AI score: 87%”
Use: “Quality check: Passed”

Instead of “Team Review”
Use: “Veroxa Review”

Instead of “Internal notes”
Use: “Client preferences” or hide completely

Instead of “Assigned Operator”
Use: remove from client view

Client Portal navigation should be simplified to:

- Overview
- Workspace
- Onboarding
- Content
- Calendar
- Media
- Google
- Reports
- Notifications

Remove “AI Agents” from Client Portal navigation.
AI Agents should only appear in Owner, Operator, or Team portals.

Content Pipeline client view should show only:
- Content title
- Content type
- Related menu item
- Status
- Suggested posting window
- Client-facing note

Do not show:
- Assigned role
- Internal stage owner
- AI score
- Internal workflow notes

Rename pipeline stages for client view:

- Received
- Being Reviewed
- Caption In Progress
- Veroxa Review
- Scheduled / Posted

Client Workspace should remove or rename:
- “Everything Veroxa knows about...” 
Replace with:
“Your restaurant profile, menu details, brand preferences, and media guidance in one place.”

If there is a Client Notes tab, rename it to:
“Preferences”

Only show client-safe preferences:
- Preferred food items to promote
- Content style preferences
- Restrictions
- Seasonal priorities

Do not show internal Veroxa notes.

Success Criteria:
- Client Portal feels clean and client-facing
- No Owner/Operator/Team/internal labels visible in Client Portal
- AI Agents removed from Client Portal navigation
- Internal dashboards remain unchanged
- App builds successfully

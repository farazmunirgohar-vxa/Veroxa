# Value Proof and Restaurant Reach Layer

Veroxa sells online presence publicly, but internally reviews whether service work is becoming cost-justifiable through relevant reach and customer-action signals.

## Signal hierarchy

Reach signals are separated from customer-action signals.

Reach includes search reach, Google/Maps reach, social reach, media reach, local customer reach quality, and action reach context.

Customer-action signals include calls, direction clicks, website clicks, menu clicks, order-link clicks, profile visits, customer mentions, social-driven visits, repeat attention, and owner-reported signals.

Reach alone is not enough. Relevant local reach and customer-action signals matter.

## Internal-only proof

Internal cost-justification can use monthly fee, average ticket, net margin, and required action/order target assumptions. Exact break-even math, requiredDailyOrders, net margin, and profit formulas must not appear on public/client pages.

Client-safe reporting may say “Customer-action signals are developing,” “More media is needed to understand what works,” and “Calls, directions, and menu clicks will become stronger proof signals when connected.”

## Safety

No live analytics, production database, real storage, live AI, connectors, payments, auto-posting, or guarantee language was added.

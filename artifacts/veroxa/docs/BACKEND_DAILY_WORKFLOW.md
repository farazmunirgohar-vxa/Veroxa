# Backend Daily Workflow

This is the worker-ready daily checklist for preview/manual Veroxa execution. It is portal-first and assumes no live systems.

## Daily checklist

1. Review the portal queue first: new client requests, media notes, confirmations, weekly update timing, and monthly report timing.
2. Check new media availability and quality. Mark whether media is usable, needs a reshoot, needs client context, or should be held.
3. Check requests and classify each as included, add-on, coming soon, not included, or confirmation required.
4. Check confirmations needed: hours, menu, prices, offers, holiday hours, website access, social access, or sensitive claims.
5. Check weekly updates: confirm what was worked on, what is pending, what media is needed, what the client needs to confirm, and what is next.
6. Check monthly reports: prepare only honest signals and limitations. Do not invent metrics.
7. Document work in the manual tracker or prepared notes so Faraz can review calmly.
8. Escalate unclear, sensitive, risky, out-of-scope, low-cooperation, or value/proof concern items to Faraz.

## Operating rules

- Portal-first: routine communication should stay in the portal.
- No routine calls/texts for normal requests.
- A 24-hour response means review/answer/next step, not guaranteed completion.
- No live publishing, no live uploads, no platform changes, and no automated customer-visible execution.
- No guarantees for orders, revenue, rankings, ROI, walk-ins, profit, customers, or growth.
- No offer invention: do not create discounts, BOGO offers, price cuts, lower prices, or new promotions.
- Confirmation required before business-truth changes or sensitive claims.

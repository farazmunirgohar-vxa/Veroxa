# Package Boundary and Portal Request Enforcement

Veroxa now has a deterministic, preview/manual-only package boundary layer for portal requests.

## Model

- Starter includes Google Business Profile support, Google Maps/local visibility basics, Facebook, Instagram, picture-based support, simple captions, media guidance/reminders, Client Portal access, and a simple monthly progress summary.
- Growth includes Starter plus Reels, TikTok, better support/stronger communication, stronger Google/local consistency, stronger content rhythm, better caption/content preparation, weekly progress updates, monthly report, and stronger portal workflow.
- Premium includes Growth plus ad management, ad planning/support, up to 1 post/day depending on usable client-provided media, stronger reporting/support, client approval, readiness assessment, and separate ad spend.

## Enforcement

Requests are classified as included, needs upgrade, needs confirmation, not supported at launch, needs team review, or unclear. Out-of-tier work is routed as an upgrade opportunity and must not be absorbed manually.

All plans block comments, DMs, inboxes, complaints, refunds, order questions, and customer-service conversations at launch. Veroxa does not invent discounts, BOGO offers, price cuts, new promotions, lower prices, or restaurant financial offers. If a restaurant already has an offer, Veroxa may ask the client to confirm exact details before preparing public copy.

## Safety

No paid/live systems were added. No production auth, real database/storage writes, live AI, platform connectors, payments, or auto-posting were added.

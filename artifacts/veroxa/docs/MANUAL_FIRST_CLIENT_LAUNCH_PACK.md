# Manual First-Client Launch Pack

Status: active source-of-truth for first-client manual/pre-live execution.

Veroxa is operating manually for the first client. The active public offer is **Complete Online Presence — $495/month**. `AUTH_MODE` remains `placeholder`; no production auth, database/storage writes, live AI, publishing connectors, payments, webhooks, cron jobs, or automated customer-visible execution are active.

## Client provides

- Google Business Profile link and access status.
- Website link and access status.
- Facebook page link and access status.
- Instagram profile link and access status.
- Menu, order, and reservation links.
- Current business hours and holiday-hour notes.
- Best sellers or priority menu items.
- Existing offers/promotions only when they already exist and the client confirms exact details.
- Usable media/photos with permission to use them.

## Veroxa handles manually

- Intake review and business-info consistency review.
- Google Business Profile, Google Maps/local visibility, website, Facebook, and Instagram readiness review.
- Website alignment/refinement when access is provided and the requested change is inside the included scope.
- Facebook/Instagram/Google update preparation from usable client media.
- Weekly update and monthly online presence report preparation.
- Veroxa team review before anything goes live.

## Veroxa does not handle

- Customer-service replies, comments, DMs, inboxes, refunds, complaints, or order questions.
- Full custom website development, hosting/domain/email troubleshooting, advanced technical SEO, ads, TikTok, Reels/video, Yelp, live integrations, automated publishing, payments, or guaranteed orders/revenue/rankings/profit/ROI/growth.

## Manual launch workflow

1. Intake review: confirm required links, access status, hours, menu/order/reservation paths, best sellers, and usable media.
2. Profile/account review: compare Google, website, Facebook, and Instagram for obvious mismatch or missing customer paths.
3. Media review: sort usable photos, weak photos, missing categories, and what Veroxa needs next.
4. Website alignment/refinement: if access is provided, prepare simple NAP/hours/menu/order/contact/social link and wording fixes only.
5. Update preparation: prepare Facebook, Instagram, and Google updates from confirmed facts and usable media.
6. Client confirmation: ask before changing business truth such as hours, menu, prices, offers, dietary/religious claims, or complaint-sensitive wording.
7. Weekly update: explain what Veroxa worked on, what was prepared/posted manually, what is pending, what media is needed, what client confirmation is needed, and what is next.
8. Monthly report: summarize handled work and observable proof without fake metrics, guarantees, ROI, profit claims, ranking claims, or invented customer results.

This launch pack is manual/pre-live. No publishing happens without Veroxa team review, and no live automation is active.

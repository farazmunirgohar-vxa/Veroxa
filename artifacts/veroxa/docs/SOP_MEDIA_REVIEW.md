# SOP — Media Review

## Review criteria

- Clarity: avoid blurry photos.
- Lighting: prefer bright, natural, appetizing light.
- Food visibility: food should be easy to understand quickly.
- Best-seller focus: ask for most popular items first.
- Storefront/menu usefulness: use for trust, location, contact, and context.
- Repeated media: save duplicates for later instead of overusing.

## When to ask for more

Ask for more media when photos are blurry/dark, no best sellers are shown, only repeated media exists, storefront/menu/context is missing, or weekly consistency is at risk.

## When to save for later

Save seasonal, duplicate, low-context, or coming-soon video material for later review.

## Confirmation required

Confirmation is required for prices, menu changes, hours, offers, catering, dietary/health claims, order links, and reservation links before public copy.

## Draft directions

- Images/photos: Facebook draft, Instagram draft, and Google Business Profile / Google update draft.
- Videos: Facebook draft, Instagram/Reels draft, Google Business Profile / Google update draft, and TikTok draft.
- Client-facing launch language: current launch is picture-based content; video/Reels/TikTok are coming soon.

## Client-safe feedback language

- Ready for Veroxa review.
- Needs clearer photo.
- More best-seller photos needed.
- Saved for later.
- Needs business confirmation.
- Useful for picture-based content.
- Video channels coming soon.

## No live systems

No real storage upload, image editing, live platform publishing, AI call, or automation is connected.

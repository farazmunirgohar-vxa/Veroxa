# SOP — Weekly Update

## Purpose

Weekly updates keep clients calm by showing what Veroxa worked on, what was posted or prepared, what is pending, what media is needed, what the client needs to confirm, and what is next.

## When prepared

Prepare once per week after reviewing portal requests, media, confirmations, website/Google/social work, and pending manual items.

## Review before writing

- Included work completed or prepared.
- Requests answered and what remains open.
- Media received and media still needed.
- Confirmations needed before public copy.
- Add-ons requested or possibly needed.
- Coming soon / not included requests.

## Include

- What Veroxa worked on.
- What was posted/prepared.
- What is pending.
- What media is needed.
- What the client needs to confirm.
- Request status language.
- Next week focus.

## Do not include

- Fake metrics.
- Guarantees.
- Internal proof math.
- Backend or technical internals.
- Offer invention.
- Live publishing claims.

## Client-safe language

- “Prepared by Veroxa.”
- “Ready for Veroxa review.”
- “Needs your input.”
- “More usable media will help keep the weekly rhythm steady.”
- “Please confirm the exact details before Veroxa prepares anything public.”
- “Portal response within 24 hours means review/answer/next step, not guaranteed completion.”

## Example weekly update template

1. **What Veroxa worked on:** reviewed Google/Maps readiness, website alignment details, media, and open portal requests.
2. **What was posted/prepared:** prepared picture-based Facebook/Instagram/Google update drafts for review when usable media exists.
3. **What is pending:** waiting on access, media, or confirmations.
4. **Media needed:** best-seller photos, storefront/dining-room photo, menu/contact photo.
5. **Confirmations needed:** hours/menu/prices if mentioned, links, existing offer details if client already has an offer.
6. **Requests:** included/add-on/coming-soon/not-included status.
7. **Next week:** finish setup, prepare safe content, and keep report baseline organized.

## No live systems

Weekly updates are manual/pre-live. No live sending, publishing, storage upload, platform connection, payment, or automation is connected.

# Pakistan Team Execution SOP

Status: active manual/pre-live execution SOP for support workers.

## Operating mode

Veroxa is manual/pre-live. Team members prepare, review, document, and flag work. They do not activate live automation, publish without Veroxa team review, connect APIs, run customer-visible automated execution, or promise Yelp/TikTok/Reels/Ads/live integrations.

## Worker responsibilities

- Review provided Google Business Profile, website, Facebook, Instagram, menu/order/reservation links, hours, best sellers, existing confirmed offers, and usable media/photos.
- Prepare draft updates using only confirmed business facts.
- Identify missing access, missing media, mismatched hours/links, weak customer paths, and items needing client confirmation.
- Use client-safe language: prepared by Veroxa, in review, needs your input, visibility update, prepared action, more content needed.

## Escalate to Faraz

- Anything unclear about business truth.
- Customer-service/DM/comment/refund/complaint/order issues.
- Requests outside Complete Online Presence — $495/month.
- Access issues.
- Bad/insufficient media.
- Yelp/TikTok/Reels/Ads/live integrations.
- Any legal, health, dietary, religious, price, discount, or serious complaint claim.

# Audit-to-First-Client Flow

## 1. Audit submitted

The restaurant submits a review-mode online presence audit request. The audit is pre-live, not a live scan, and recommendations are not guarantees.

## 2. Manual review

Faraz reviews fit, business type, online presence gaps, media likelihood, access likelihood, and whether Veroxa can help within Complete Online Presence — $495/month.

## 3. Fit decision

- **Complete Online Presence fit:** proceed to onboarding explanation.
- **Not ready / manual review:** ask for clarifying details or delay.
- **Not fit yet:** politely decline or park until boundaries match.

## 4. Explain offer

Explain one offer: Complete Online Presence — $495/month with Google Business Profile support, Google Maps/local visibility basics, local SEO/search basics, website alignment if access is provided, Facebook, Instagram, picture-based content, up to 3 posts/updates per week media dependent, weekly updates, monthly online presence report, portal request response/review/answer within 24 hours, and Veroxa team review before anything goes live.

## 5. Explain boundaries

No guarantees. No customer-service replies, comments, DMs, inboxes, refunds, complaints, order questions, full custom website development, hosting/domain/email troubleshooting, Yelp/TikTok/Reels/Ads yet, daily posting, automated publishing, or live integrations.

## 6. Explain add-ons

New basic website +$95 and missing Facebook/Instagram profile creation +$45/profile. Yelp setup is coming soon and not a launch add-on.

## 7. Onboarding expectations

Client acknowledges what Veroxa does, what Veroxa does not handle, what restaurant provides, what details must be confirmed, what 24-hour response means, what is not guaranteed, add-ons, and coming-soon items.

## 8. Collect access/media

Collect Google Business Profile link/access, website link/access, Facebook link/access, Instagram link/access, best-seller photos, storefront/menu/contact photos, and confirmed business details.

## 9. Begin weekly update cycle

Prepare weekly update manually: work completed/prepared, pending items, media needed, confirmations needed, request status, next week focus.

## 10. First monthly report baseline

Prepare baseline report after enough manual context exists: work handled, Google/Maps/local visibility, website alignment, Facebook/Instagram, media, reach/action signals, limitations, and next month focus.

## No payment implementation

This flow does not implement checkout, subscriptions, billing, payment automation, or live systems.

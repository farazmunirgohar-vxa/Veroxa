# Backend Weekly Update Playbook

Weekly updates are short, calm, and practical. They tell the client what Veroxa handled, what is pending, what media is needed, what the client needs to confirm, and what happens next.

## Preparation checklist

- Review the week’s portal requests and media.
- Confirm what was posted, prepared, reviewed, or held for later manual execution.
- Note anything waiting on media or client confirmation.
- Check request statuses and add-on/coming-soon/not-included boundaries.
- Prepare draft wording and send to Faraz/team review before client delivery.

## Example weekly update

> This week Veroxa reviewed your Google/Maps basics, checked your website/menu/contact alignment, and prepared Facebook/Instagram picture-based content from the usable media available. We are waiting on brighter food photos before preparing more posts. Please confirm whether your weekend hours and menu link are still current. Next, Veroxa will continue local visibility upkeep and prepare the next content items after media review.

## Media-needed wording

- “More usable food, menu, storefront, and dining-area photos will help Veroxa keep weekly updates consistent.”
- “Posting may slow when usable media is not available.”

## Confirmation-needed wording

- “Please confirm the exact hours/menu/price/offer details before Veroxa prepares public-facing copy.”
- “Veroxa will hold this item until the restaurant confirms the business details.”

## Request-status wording

- Included: “This fits your Complete Online Presence service and is in review.”
- Add-on: “This is available as an add-on if you want Veroxa to prepare it.”
- Coming soon: “This is not included at launch and will be considered later.”
- Not included: “This is outside Veroxa’s launch scope.”

## What not to say

- Do not promise orders, rankings, revenue, ROI, growth, walk-ins, profit, or customers.
- Do not invent discounts, BOGO offers, price cuts, lower prices, or new promotions.
- Do not claim live publishing, live integrations, or automatic execution.
- Do not expose internal proof math.

Faraz/team review is required before the update is delivered.

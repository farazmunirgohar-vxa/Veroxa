# First Week Execution Checklist

Status: active first-week manual execution checklist.

## Day 1 — Intake review

- Confirm access/info for Google Business Profile, website, Facebook, Instagram, menu/order/reservation links, business hours, best sellers, existing confirmed offers, and usable media/photos.
- Note missing items and ask the client for the smallest next action.

## Days 2–3 — Profile/account and media review

- Review Google/Maps, website, Facebook, and Instagram for consistency and customer paths.
- Sort media into usable, needs cropping/context, not usable, and missing categories.
- Escalate access issues or unclear business truth to Faraz.

## Days 3–5 — Prep updates and alignment

- Prepare website alignment/refinement if access is provided and the edit is in included scope.
- Prepare Facebook, Instagram, and Google updates using confirmed business facts and usable media.
- Ask for confirmation before hours, prices, menu, offers, or sensitive claims change.

## End of week — Weekly update

Send a calm weekly update covering what Veroxa worked on, what was posted/prepared manually, what is pending, what media is needed, what the client needs to confirm, and what is next.

Required workflow markers: Media review; Website alignment/refinement.

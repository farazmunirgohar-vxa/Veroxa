# Portal Request SLA — 24-Hour Response Model

The Veroxa portal is the normal channel for routine client requests. Clients should submit routine work inside the portal instead of texting/calling for day-to-day work.

## SLA promise

Veroxa responds within 24 hours. This means an answer, review status, client-input request, upgrade route, not-included note, not-supported note, completion note, or manual-work scheduling note.

It does not mean every piece of work is completed within 24 hours.

## Statuses

Portal requests can be received, in review, needs client input, upgrade required, scheduled for manual work, completed, declined, not supported, escalated, or paused.

Team surfaces show SLA due, due soon, overdue, needs response, upgrade route, and confirmation needed. Client pages use simple language such as “Your request is in review” and “Veroxa will respond within 24 hours.”

## Safety

No webhooks, cron jobs, background jobs, production auth, real storage, live AI, database writes, payments, or automated customer-visible execution were added.

# Faraz Escalation Rules

Status: active escalation rules for manual first-client service.

Escalate to Faraz before action when any of these appear:

- Business truth is unclear: hours, holiday hours, menu, prices, discounts, offers, catering availability, dietary/religious claims, health/legal claims, ownership details, or serious complaint context.
- Customer-service issues: replies, comments, DMs, inboxes, refunds, complaints, order questions, or angry customer situations.
- Request is outside the current package: full custom website development, hosting/domain/email troubleshooting, advanced technical SEO, ads, TikTok, Reels/video, Yelp, live integrations, automated publishing, payment/billing, or guaranteed results.
- Access issues: missing/incorrect Google Business Profile, website, Facebook, or Instagram access.
- Media issues: bad/insufficient media, unclear permission, old menu photos, blurry content, or media that could misrepresent the restaurant.
- Any customer-visible action that has not had Veroxa team review.

Default decision: hold for review, ask for confirmation, or prepare a safe draft. Do not publish automatically.

Required escalation markers: Requests outside current scope; Bad/insufficient media.

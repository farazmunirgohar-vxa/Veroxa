# Free Audit Future Note

The public Free Audit should remain a safe prospect-facing lead tool. Output
copy must avoid internal implementation terms and should not imply live account
access, automatic publishing, guaranteed rankings, or ad results.

Future path, when explicitly requested:

1. Prospect completes the public audit.
2. Veroxa stores or imports the lead through an approved write path.
3. Team Audit Leads shows the audit lead for Faraz review.
4. Faraz decides whether to follow up, prepare recommendations, or ignore.

No automation claim is active today. The audit lead-to-team queue should be a
future reviewed handoff, not a promise that Veroxa automatically acts on public
submissions.

# SOP — Monthly Report

## Purpose

The monthly online presence report explains what Veroxa handled, what appears to be working, what needs improvement, what media is needed, and what the next month should focus on.

## Sections

- What Veroxa handled.
- Google/Maps/local visibility review.
- Website alignment review.
- Facebook/Instagram content review.
- Media used.
- Media working/not-working.
- Reach/action signals.
- Limitations.
- Next month focus.

## Reach/action signals

Client-safe signals include calls, directions, website/menu/order clicks, profile actions, content reach, customer mentions, and owner-reported online-influenced activity. Early reports may say not enough data yet.

## Do not include

- Internal proof math.
- Fake metrics.
- Guarantee language.
- Claims that Veroxa generated sales.
- Live analytics claims if live systems are not connected.

## Example report structure

1. Summary.
2. What Veroxa handled.
3. Google/Maps/local visibility progress.
4. Website alignment progress.
5. Facebook/Instagram picture-based content progress.
6. Media review: what worked and what did not.
7. Reach/action signal review.
8. Limitations and missing inputs.
9. Next month focus.

## No live systems

Reports are manual/pre-live until paid/live systems are separately approved. No live analytics, database, storage, integrations, payments, or automated reporting are connected.

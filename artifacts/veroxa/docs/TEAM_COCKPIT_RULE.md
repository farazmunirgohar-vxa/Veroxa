# Team Portal Cockpit Rule

The Team Portal is the powerful Veroxa engine with a simple steering wheel.

The Team Dashboard should open as a calm mobile-first Today View that answers:

1. What needs my attention today?
2. What should I open first?
3. What is blocked or waiting on the client?
4. Are reports, approvals, or readiness items needing review?

The top of `/team/dashboard` should prioritize:

- Start here
- Needs review
- Ready to schedule
- Client requests
- Blocked / needs input
- Reports due
- Approvals ready
- First-client readiness

Queue surfaces should feel related by showing a consistent shape where practical: client, item type, status, next action, priority, due timing, and a clear action button.

Do not turn the Team Portal into an inactive-role dashboard, super-admin console, analytics wall, overwhelming operations screen, or generic shortcut-card page.

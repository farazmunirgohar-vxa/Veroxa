# Team Cockpit Rule

The Team Portal should feel like a powerful engine with a simple steering wheel.

Keep the dashboard centered on five operating questions:

1. What needs review?
2. What is ready to schedule?
3. What did a client request?
4. What is blocked or needs client input?
5. What reports are due?

Avoid adding Owner, Operator, Super Admin, command-center, analytics-wall, or backend-console language to active Team Portal routes. Deeper queues may show operational detail, but the dashboard should stay calm and action-focused.
